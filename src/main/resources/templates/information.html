Handy

Created by Björn Borgar Magnússon, Fannar Steinn Aðalsteinsson, Kári Kjærnested og Kári Viðar Jónsson

About

        Handy is an application which is made for people in need of a helping hand in the shape of specialized handyman or a craftsman.
        Also is it a space for those specialized craftsmen to advertise themselves.
